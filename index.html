Daihong Zhou (周代宏) is a Ph.D student in School of Computer Science of Fudan University, Shanghai, China. He received his master degree in China University of Mining and Technology (Beijing) in Beijing, China.

Dr. Zhou's research focuses on software data analysis, program analysi, code LLM, and technical debt.

Collaboration Invitation: Dr. Zhou is open to collaborating with professors, researchers, and industry professionals who share similar research interests or have relevant projects. If you are interested in exploring opportunities for teamwork, please don't hesitate to reach out via email at dhzhou17@fudan.edu.cn for potential collaborations.
