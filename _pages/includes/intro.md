    Dr. Zhou's research focuses on <strong><u>AI for Software Engineering (AI4SE)</u></strong>, <strong><u>Software Quality Assurance (SQA)</u></strong> and <strong><u>Robotics Software Engineering (RoSE)</u></strong>, with particular emphasis on their applications in both traditional software systems and robotic software systems. He explores how advanced AI techniques, such as large language models (LLMs) and knowledge graphs (KGs), can enhance development efficiency, quality assurance, and system reliability. In addition, Dr. Zhou addresses challenges in robotic motion control and planning, leveraging LLMs and vision-language models (VLMs) to improve robotic automation. He aims to combine software engineering principles and AI technologies to drive intelligent and reliable autonomous systems.
