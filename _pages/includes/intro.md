<div style="text-align:justify; text-justify:inter-word; margin:10px">
    Dr. Zhou’s research focuses on <strong>software engineering</strong>, <strong>software quality assurance</strong>, and <strong>robotics software engineering</strong>. His primary interest lies in applying intelligent technologies, such as large language models (LLMs) and knowledge graphs (KGs), to solve multi-dimensional data analysis challenges in software engineering / robotic system, enhancing intelligent development and quality assurance.
